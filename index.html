About Me

Background

Growing up on a remote moisture farm, found on a far flung desert planet, I learned the value of hard work beneath the blistering binary stars of my homeworld.


Recent Challenges

After a vist from a disguised space-wizard, who brought the promise of adventure and the hope of finding out more about my birth parents, I was convinced me to travel beyond the comfort of what I knew.


Future Goals

I have recently begun the journey to discover and meet my birth father and although I have heard rumours about his temper, I am optimistic that the family reunion will be emotional and memorable.
